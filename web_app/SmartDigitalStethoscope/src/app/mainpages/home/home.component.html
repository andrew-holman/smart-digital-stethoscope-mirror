The new way to keep
your heart in check




Welcome to Smart-Digital Stethoscope. This device is the newest way to check you heart and communicate with your health care professional. This stethoscope allows the patient to take a recording of their own heartbeat which is uploaded to their private account on our webapp. Your health care professional can then access this file and to determine the next part in your health journey. It also provides a system that will flag and recordings that appear to indicate possible heart problems such as murmurs. Our goal is to help provide the best care possible for all.
